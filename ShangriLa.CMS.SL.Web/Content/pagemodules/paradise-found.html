Paradise found

Nestled in 15 acres of landscaped gardens filled with exotic flowers, waterfalls and koi-filled ponds, Shangri-La Hotel, Singapore leaves city life behind in favour of natural tranquility.

Offering 747 rooms and suites across three distinct wings, including 127 serviced apartments and condominium units, Shangri-La Hotel, Singapore is the original Shangri-La hotel, first opening its doors in 1971. Located only a short walk from Singapore’s main shopping, dining and entertainment district, the hotel is ideally located for business and leisure travel. Consistently voted one of the world’s best hotels, the hotel upholds our tradition of Asian hospitality and service, set amid incredible natural beauty.
